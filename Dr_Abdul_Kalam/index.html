Dr.Abdul kalam

Abdul kalam was an Indian aerospace research scientist and politician

Here's a time line of Dr.Abdul kalam life:

  • 1931-Born in Rameswaram
  • 1954-Graduates in physics
  • 1960-Degree in Aeronautical Engineering
  • 1969-Transferred to ISRO from the DRDO
  • 1970-Projects Devil and Project Valiant
  • 1975-Worked in Ramani Rangaro of Vijayawada
  • 1980-Transfered to Indian Space Research Organisation
  • 1981-Padma Vibushan
  • 1988-Prithvi missile
  • 1990-Padma Bushan
  • 1992-Chief Scientific Advicer
  • 2002-President of India
  • 2015-Passed away
